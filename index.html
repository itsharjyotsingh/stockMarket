  • Stock exchanges
  • Stock indices
  • Buying and selling stocks
  • Stock trading
  • Dividends
  • Stock splits
  • Order types
  • Reading stock quotes
  • Ticker symbols
  • Key market participants

Course Overview: This course is designed for those who have completed the basic course or have some experience in the stock market. It covers advanced concepts such as:

  • Stock market analysis
  • Stock market research
  • Stock market valuation
  • Stock market trends
  • Stock market indicators
  • Stock market data
  • Impact of psychological biases on investment decisions
  • Market anomalies and investor behavior
  • Strategies to mitigate behavioral biases
